About

I've built this site to help myself keep up with news and government.

  • News: The majority of stories on a news site are there just to fill space. I've never seen more than 2 relevant stories on a site at a time. This page takes the top 2 stories from neutral news sources, then uses NLP to create a summary of the stories.
  • Supreme Court Rulings: Gives all of the ruling from the last 3 days worth of rulings. The links on this page go directly to the justices' opinions on the case. The majority of these ruling are boring, pedantic, legalistic arguments, but I like to keep an eye out for important rulings. There are the occassional interesting philosophical arguments.
  • Congressional Bills: Gives the last 25 bills that have passed at least one house of congress.
